My Favorite Superheroes: A Semantic Webpage

DC Heroes

DC superhero comics are the best.
The storylines are more complex and often address real life social issues. My favorite is Green Arrow.

Marvel Heroes

Marvel superhero comics feel more two dimensional; however, Marvel is really killing it in the film and tv industries, where DC's attempts to capitalize on their success are more mediocre.

Conclusion

To summarize, Marvel heroes are not as cool as DC heroes, but they do have decent movies and tv shows (whatever that's worth).

It's worth noting here that I'm actually a Marvel fan anyway.
