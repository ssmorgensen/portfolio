Humble Beginnings

Early Life

Though it is unknown where Fio was born, she discovered her Humans at the NOAH Center in Stanwood, Washington in 2004. At that time she was a mere kitten,
stuck in a room with so many other kittens. Though she doesn't consider herself lucky to have been chosen as a companion, she has been quoted as saying,
"Meow meow mew rawr," which is enough for this author to understand the sentiment.

Fio was not alone in being brought home that fateful day, as her adoptive brother JiJi also tagged along. Though not partcularly useful, JiJi did make the
initial contact with the Human (hereafter referred to as "Human 2"). Once JiJi had been chosen, shelter staff recommended that a second cat be chosen as
well, stating, "That cat is needy and we recommend bringing him home with a friend." Though on arrival at home Human 1 would accuse Human 2 of being upsold,
she quickly realized that indeed JiJi was the neediest of cats, and besides the obvious reasons (such as her beauty, grace, and superior intelligence),
it was a good thing that Fio was brought home, if nothing else but to provide JiJi with a distraction.
